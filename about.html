About // The Black Cat Cafe

We save cats

The Black Cat Cafe is a cafe with a mission:

PALS (The Pet Adoption and Lifecare Society) is a nonprofit 501c3 organization dedicated to rescuing pets whose only option is life on the streets or euthanasia at a high-kill shelter. PALS purchased the café in March of 2011 with the goal of providing a superb dining experience in its own right and donating every dime to save lives. Last year alone 1000 homeless pets got a second chance at life and love through PALS. With the addition of the café and gift shop, we hope to double that number within a year. If you are going to eat out, why not do something good at the same time?
