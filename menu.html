Good food for a good cause

Our focus is on quality homemade food, prepared on the premises by our certified and seasoned staff. Either take-out or in-house, we feature great salads, sandwiches, soups and quiches, luscious desserts and homemade baked goods, such as scones and cinnamon rolls. Or enjoy a cup of espresso or cappuccino with free wifi.
